fixture, its artifact-pointer process-abort child entry point, and a pre-existing

omitted scenarios.

schema, parse successfully as JSON.  Every registered =agent-semantic-client-db=
benchmark target compiles through =--all-targets=, and each new performance
scenario has an executed receipt in this RFC directory.
